Tour - Basic Concepts

  • Slides are individual HTML files, prefetched at runtime
  • Slides can be cached (in production mode) for faster loading times
  • The config file is the second-most important part (after the slides themselves, of course) - it controls many aspects of the overall presentation
  • There's also a dashboard to see how long things take to load, and a preview page for the slides that does the same thing (these can be accessed by pressing the d and s keys, respectively).
  • Moulin is bare-bones, making it easily customisable (see the global stylesheet config key)
  • Moulin emits 10 hooks to provide granular control for over-the-top effects
